# Prompt v4.8 - Current Version

This is the current recommended prompt template for investment report generation.

## Key Features

- **Executive Summary First**: Verdict and key insights at the top
- **Dynamic Headers**: Section headers include ticker-specific numbers
- **Plain English**: Zero-tolerance jargon policy with translations
- **~2,500 Word Target**: Approximately 10-minute read time

## Target Audience

Your reader is a 25-35 year old who:
- Has $50-500/month to invest
- May have student loans
- Wants to build wealth responsibly
- Knows what stocks are but doesn't understand financial statements

## Report Structure

### Part 1: Executive Summary

1. **TL;DR** - 2-3 punchy sentences with one-line business description, key insight, and recommendation
2. **What Do They Do?** - 2-3 sentences making the business tangible
3. **Quick Health Check** - Report card table with 🟢/🟡/🟠/🔴 flags
4. **Investment Fit Assessment** - Table for different investor types
5. **The Verdict** - Rating table with star ratings and overall BUY/HOLD/SELL

### Part 2: Detailed Analysis

6. **Growth** - 5-year story, recent quarters, growth sources
7. **Profitability** - Margins, efficiency, industry comparison
8. **Valuation** - Mean reversion analysis, ROE/ROA, analyst expectations
9. **Earnings Quality** - Real vs adjusted profit, stock-based compensation
10. **Cash Flow** - Quarterly cash flow, cash quality, CapEx breakdown
11. **Debt** - 5-year debt story, payment timeline, personal finance analogy
12. **Dilution** - Share count changes, buyback analysis
13. **Bull Case** - 3-4 optimistic points
14. **Bear Case** - 3-4 cautious points
15. **Warning Signs** - Checklist with flags
16. **6-Point Vibe Check** - Final assessment before ratings

### Part 3: Real Talk

17. **Real Talk** - Casual recommendation with honest risk assessment

## Section Header Format

All section headers MUST use: `## Static Keyword: Dynamic Subtitle`

| Section | Format | Example |
|---------|--------|---------|
| Quick Health Check | `## Quick Health Check: {ticker}'s [Year] Report Card` | `## Quick Health Check: NVO's 2026 Report Card` |
| Growth | `## Growth: [X% trend or story]` | `## Growth: From X% to Y% — The Slowdown Story` |
| Profitability | `## Profitability: [Margin % or story]` | `## Profitability: X% Margins — The Profit Machine` |
| Valuation | `## Valuation: [X% vs history]` | `## Valuation: X% Off — Historic Discount` |
| Cash Flow | `## Cash Flow: [$X or cash story]` | `## Cash Flow: The $XB Cash Machine` |
| Debt | `## Debt: [$X or debt story]` | `## Debt: The $XB War Chest` |

## Jargon Translations

| Wall Street Says | You Say |
|------------------|---------|
| Free Cash Flow | money left over |
| FCF Margin | what they keep |
| Operating Cash Flow | cash that actually came in |
| Debt-to-Equity | how much they borrowed |
| Interest Coverage | can they pay their loans? |
| Net Debt | what they still owe |
| Net Cash | extra savings after debt |
| Gross Margin | profit before overhead |
| Operating Margin | profit after running costs |
| Net Margin | actual take-home |
| Stock-Based Compensation | paying staff with stock |
| Dilution | your slice shrinks |
| EPS | profit per share |

## Verdict Weighting

- **50%**: Recent 2-4 quarters (momentum matters most)
- **30%**: 5-year trajectory
- **20%**: Balance sheet + earnings quality

- [ ] Business analogy introduced early and echoed throughout
