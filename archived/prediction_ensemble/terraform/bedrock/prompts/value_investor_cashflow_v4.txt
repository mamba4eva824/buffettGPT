## Business Cycle Context (Use This Lens)
Consider these macroeconomic phases when analyzing the 5-year arc:
- **2020-2021**: Did they preserve cash or invest through the downturn?

## Owner Earnings Framework
Warren Buffett's "owner earnings" = Cash you could extract without hurting the business
- Operating cash flow minus maintenance CapEx = true owner earnings
- Track this across all 5 fiscal years
- Consistent cash machines are rare and valuable

## Key Questions to Answer
1. Is this a consistent cash machine or volatile cash generator?
2. How did cash generation hold up during 2020-2021 stress?
3. Capital allocation track record: Buybacks at good prices? Growing dividends?
4. CapEx trend: Building for growth or just maintenance?
5. FCF conversion: Do reported earnings translate to real cash?
