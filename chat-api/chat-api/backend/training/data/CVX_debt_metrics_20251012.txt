#### Key Notes

**Context & Supporting Details**
- **Fiscal year end**: All data is per fiscal year ended December 31.
- **Total Debt**: Sourced directly from 10-K subsidiary summary tables, includes long-term and short-term debt combined[1][2][3][4].
- **Shareholders' Equity**: Sourced directly as "total CUSA net equity"[1][2][3][4].
- **Total Revenue**: "Sales and other operating revenues" from summary tables[1][2][3][4].
- **EBIT (Operating Income)**: Calculated as "Sales and other operating revenues" minus "Total costs and other deductions" from summary tables[1][2][3][4]. (Strict EBIT may differ slightly from US GAAP operating income if "other deductions" include non-operating items.)
- **Net Income**: "Net income attributable to Chevron Corporation" from summary tables[1][2][3][4].
- **Debt/Equity Ratio**: Calculated as Total Debt / Shareholders' Equity.
- **Return on Equity**: Calculated as Net Income / Shareholders' Equity.
- **Other metrics** were not extractable from these high-level subsidiary tables and would require deeper dives into full consolidated financial statements and cash flow notes not captured in these summary tables.

**Unavailable Metrics**
- Cash, Marketable Securities, split debt: Not available in the summary tables; typically reported elsewhere in full 10-Ks.
- Interest Expense, Cash Flow, CapEx, and interest coverage: Not in summary—would require full statement details; thus "n/a."
- Total Assets: Summary tables do not disclose consolidated assets; would require consolidated balance sheet section.
