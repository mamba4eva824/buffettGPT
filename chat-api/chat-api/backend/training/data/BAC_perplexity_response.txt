## Where to Find Each Metric in the 10-K

- **Balance Sheet**: Consolidated Balance Sheet (Assets, Liabilities, Equity)
- **Total Assets**: Sum of all assets.
- **Total Debt**: Long-term debt + Short-term borrowings (often under “Borrowings”).
- **Total Equity**: Total shareholders’ equity.
- **Cash & Equivalents**: Listed under current assets.
- **Accounts Receivable**: For banks, often “Loans” or “Financing Receivables”; traditional “A/R” is rare.
- **Inventory**: Not typically reported by banks.
- **PP&E (net)**: Property, plant, and equipment, net of depreciation (usually minor for banks).
- **Income Statement**: Consolidated Statement of Income
- **Total Revenue**: Net interest income + noninterest income.
- **Cost of Revenue**: Not a standard bank metric; for banks, focus on “Noninterest Expense.”
- **Gross Profit**: Not standard; for banks, use “Net Interest Income + Noninterest Income - Noninterest Expense.”
- **Operating Income (EBIT)**: Income before income taxes + interest expense.
- **Interest Expense**: Interest on deposits and borrowings.
- **Net Income**: Bottom-line profit.
- **Diluted EPS**: Earnings per share, diluted.
- **Shares Outstanding (diluted)**: Weighted average diluted shares.
- **Cash Flow Statement**: Consolidated Statement of Cash Flows
- **Operating Cash Flow**: Net cash provided by operating activities.
- **Capital Expenditures**: Purchases of premises and equipment.
- **Free Cash Flow**: Operating cash flow - CapEx.
- **Dividends Paid**: Cash dividends paid.
- **Stock Buybacks**: Common stock repurchased.
- **Key Ratios**: Calculate from above metrics.
- **Debt-to-Equity**: Total Debt / Total Equity.
- **Interest Coverage**: EBIT / Interest Expense.
- **ROE**: Net Income / Average Equity.
- **ROA**: Net Income / Average Assets.
- **Net Margin**: Net Income / Total Revenue.
- **FCF Margin**: Free Cash Flow / Total Revenue.
