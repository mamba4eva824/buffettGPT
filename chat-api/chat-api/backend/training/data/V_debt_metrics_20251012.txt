### Data Sources and Calculation Notes

- Fiscal Year End Date is **September 30** each year[2][3][4].
- **Total Assets, Total Debt, Long-term Debt, Short-term Debt, Total Equity, Cash & Equiv., Marketable Securities** from Balance Sheet section of the annual report[2][3][4].
- **Total Revenue, EBIT, Interest Expense, Net Income** from Income Statement, “Consolidated Statements of Operations”[2][3][4].
- **Operating Cash Flow, CapEx** from “Consolidated Statement of Cash Flows”[2][3][4].
- **Free Cash Flow** calculated as Operating Cash Flow minus CapEx.
- **Debt-to-Equity = Total Debt / Total Equity**.
- **Interest Coverage = EBIT / Interest Expense**.
- **Net Debt = Total Debt – Cash & Equiv.** (ignores marketable securities for conservative standard view).
- **Return on Equity (ROE) = Net Income / Equity**.
- **Return on Assets (ROA) = Net Income / Total Assets**.
