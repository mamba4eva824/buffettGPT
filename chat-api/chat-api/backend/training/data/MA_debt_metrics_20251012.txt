### **Metric Definitions & Data Sources**
- **Total Assets, Debt, Equity, Cash, Securities:** Extracted from the balance sheets in each 10-K filing[1][2][3][4][5].
- **Long-term Debt & Short-term Debt:** Reported as such in balance sheet footnotes[1][2][3][4][5].
- **Total Debt:** Long-term + Short-term/Current Portion of LTD[1][2][3][4][5].
- **Revenues, EBIT, Net Income, Interest:** Income statement lines (Operating Income ≈ EBIT)[1][2][3][4][5].
- **Operating Cash Flow, CapEx:** Consolidated cash flow statements[1][2][3][4][5].
- **Free Cash Flow:** Operating CF – CapEx (calculated).
- **Marketable Securities:** Reported separately where available; if not, marked as "--"[1][2][3][4][5].
- **Debt/Equity Ratio:** Total Debt / Equity (calculated).
- **Interest Coverage Ratio:** EBIT / Interest Exp (calculated).
- **Net Debt:** (Total Debt – Cash & Equivalents) (calculated).
- **ROE (Return on Equity):** Net Income / Equity (calculated).
- **ROA (Return on Assets):** Net Income / Total Assets (calculated).
