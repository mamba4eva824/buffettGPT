## Key Observations

**Services Growth:** Services revenue showed the strongest growth trajectory, increasing 12.9% year-over-year in FY 2024, driven primarily by advertising, the App Store, and cloud services[1]. This segment achieved a gross margin of 73.9% in FY 2024, significantly higher than the Products gross margin of 37.2%[1].

**Product Performance:** iPhone revenue remained relatively flat during 2024 compared to 2023[1]. Mac revenue increased 2% primarily due to higher laptop sales, while iPad decreased 6% due to lower sales of iPad Pro and entry-level models[1].

**Profitability Trends:** Despite a 2% increase in revenue, net income decreased by 3.4% from $96,995 million in FY 2023 to $93,736 million in FY 2024[1], resulting in a slight compression of the net profit margin from 25.31% to 23.97%.

**Capital Structure:** Apple's negative retained earnings position has deepened from ($214) million in FY 2023 to ($19,154) million in FY 2024[1], primarily driven by aggressive share repurchases totaling $95,846 million in FY 2024[1].
