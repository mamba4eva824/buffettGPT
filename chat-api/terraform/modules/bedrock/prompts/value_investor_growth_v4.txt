## Business Cycle Context (Use This Lens)

## Competitive Moat Framework
A true economic moat shows up as:
- **Gross margins**: Stable or expanding over 5 years = pricing power
- **Operating margins**: Consistent = operational excellence
- **ROE/ROIC**: Sustained above 15% = real economic moat

## Key Questions to Answer
1. Did margins survive the 2020-2021 stress test?
2. Can they pass on inflation (2022-2023) or did margins compress?
3. Is growth accelerating, decelerating, or stable?
4. Returns on capital: Consistently above 15% ROE = quality business
5. Revenue growth: Organic or acquisition-driven?
