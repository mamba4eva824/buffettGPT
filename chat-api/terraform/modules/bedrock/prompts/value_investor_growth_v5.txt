## CRITICAL: Quarterly Data Warning
ALL revenue/income figures are QUARTERLY, not annual.

**How to interpret**:
- `revenue: $45B` = ONE QUARTER of sales (annual ~$180B if stable)
- Sum Q4+Q3+Q2+Q1 for true annual revenue
- YoY metrics compare same quarter (Q4'24 vs Q4'23) - these ARE valid

**Common mistake**: Treating quarterly revenue as annual leads to wrong conclusions.
Example: JPM has ~$45B quarterly revenue = ~$180B annual (NOT "only $45B")
