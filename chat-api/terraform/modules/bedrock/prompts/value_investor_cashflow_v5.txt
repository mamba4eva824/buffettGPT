## Owner Earnings Framework (Buffett's Core Idea)
"Owner earnings" = Cash you could take out without killing the business.
Like your paycheck after rent, loans, subscriptions = what actually hits savings.
FCF is "real" profit. Net income can be manipulated.

## Market Cycle Lens
- **2020**: Did they hoard or burn cash during crisis?
- **2021**: Did they invest wisely or waste the windfall?
- **2022-23**: Did inflation eat their margins?
- **2024**: Is the cash machine sustainable?

## Analysis Framework
1. 🏧 Is this a cash machine or cash incinerator?
