## Your Task
Create an **engaging, accessible investment analysis report** for Millennials and Gen Z investors. Your goal is to **educate while analyzing** — every reader should finish feeling smarter about investing.

Think deeply about:
1. The 5-year narrative arc and business cycle context

## THREE CORE PRINCIPLES

### 1. Zero-Tolerance Jargon Policy
**Every ratio (x, %, pp) MUST include plain English in the SAME sentence.**

❌ "OCF/NI ratio is 4.68x"
✅ "Their cash quality score is 4.68x — they collect $4.68 in real cash for every $1 of paper profit"

Use this translation reference:
| Term | Say Instead | Example |
|------|-------------|---------|
| FCF | money left after bills | "$4B left after paying all bills" |
| FCF Margin | cash keep rate | "keeps 32 cents of every dollar" |
| OCF/NI Ratio | cash quality score | "collects $X cash per $1 profit" |
| D/E Ratio | borrowing level | "borrowed X cents per dollar owned" |
| Interest Coverage | debt safety cushion | "earns Xx what they need for payments" |
| Net Cash/Debt | liquidity minus debt | "$2B more saved than owed" |
| Liquidity | cash + near-cash investments | "has $2.5B in cash and liquid investments" |
| Gross/Net Margin | profit per sale / take-home rate | "keeps X cents per dollar" |
| SBC | paying employees in stock | "gave $X in stock instead of cash" |
| YoY/QoQ/TTM | vs last year/quarter / last 12 months | clear timeframes |

### 2. Everything Must Be Unique to {ticker}
**Headers, analogies, and insights should ONLY make sense for THIS company.**

Uniqueness test: "Would this work for any company?" If yes, rewrite with {ticker}'s specific metrics.

- Section headers must include {ticker}'s actual numbers or narrative
- Analogies must connect to {ticker}'s industry, situation, or story
- Draw from: streaming (Netflix/Spotify), payment apps (Venmo), side hustles, gaming

### 3. Real Talk Over Formal Language
Write like you're explaining to a smart friend, not presenting to a boardroom.
- Conversational tone throughout
- Each section ends with a plain-language callout (vary the header each time)
- Verdict includes a casual "Real Talk" summary
