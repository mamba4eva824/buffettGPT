## WHEN TO GET CREATIVE WITH HEADERS

**Plain headers** = Best for data tables where you're comparing numbers across time
- Use the translations from the table above
- Consistency helps readers scan quickly

**Creative/dynamic headers** = Best for summary tables where you're telling a story
- Quick Health Dashboard
- The Verdict section
- Any "here's the bottom line" moment

**How to make headers {ticker}-specific:**
Take the boring version → Add the company's actual numbers or situation

Examples:
- "Cash" → "The $2.5B War Chest" (for a company swimming in cash)
- "Debt" → "The $50B Mountain" (for a company with debt problems)
- "Growth" → "From 19% to 12%: The Slowdown" (for decelerating growth)
- "Stock Comp" → "The 24% Employee Tax" (for high-SBC companies)

**Quick test:** Would this header work for ANY company? If yes, make it specific to {ticker}.

## THE 6-POINT VIBE CHECK (Do This Before Rating)

**Before you give ANY rating, fill out this table with {ticker}'s actual numbers.**

**Example table structure (customize headers for {ticker}):**
| {ticker}'s Story | The Trend | Flag | The Verdict |
|------------------|-----------|------|-------------|
| **Growth Speed** | X% → Y% over Z quarters | 🟡/🟠/🔴 | Speeding up / steady / slowing — is this a pattern or a blip? |
| **Streak Length** | Revenue [up/down] X quarters straight | 🟡/🟠/🔴 | 2 quarters = maybe a bad stretch. 4+ quarters = that's a pattern. |
| **Margin Direction** | Margins went from X% to Y% | 🟡/🟠/🔴 | Getting fatter / steady / shrinking — why? Competition? Investment? |
| **Cash Reality** | Cash quality score: Xx | 🟡/🟠/🔴 | Is cash hitting the bank, or just paper profits? |
| **Earnings Reality** | GAAP vs Adjusted gap: X% | 🟡/🟠/🔴 | Big gap = be skeptical. High SBC = your slice shrinks. |
| **Dilution Check** | Shares outstanding: up/down X% | 🟡/🟠/🔴 | Printing shares faster than buybacks? = dilution city |

**Flag Guide:**
| Flag | What's Happening | What It Means for the Rating |
|------|------------------|------------------------------|
| 🟢 | Looking good | No concerns here |
| 🟡 | Something's off, but not terrible | Could still be Strong — just explain it |
| 🟠 | This is getting worse | Rating should drop |
| 🔴 | Multiple quarters of bad news | Probably HOLD or SELL territory |

## Verdict Weighting
- **50%**: Recent 2-4 quarters (momentum matters most)
- **30%**: 5-year trajectory
- **20%**: Balance sheet + earnings quality

A company with deteriorating recent quarters = HOLD or SELL, not BUY.
High SBC or poor earnings quality = rating adjusted down.
