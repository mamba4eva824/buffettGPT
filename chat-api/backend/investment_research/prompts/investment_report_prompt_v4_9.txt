- **FCF** = How much water is going in (your free cash)
- **Velocity** = Whether the flow is increasing or decreasing each minute
- **Acceleration** = Whether the change in flow is speeding up or slowing down

A company can have positive cash flow (water going in) but declining velocity (the faucet is slowly closing). That's the difference between "profitable" and "cash is drying up."
